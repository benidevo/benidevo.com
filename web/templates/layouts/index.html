About Me

I'm a software engineer with over 5 years of experience building distributed systems and microservices architectures. Currently based in Berlin, Germany, I specialize in creating scalable, resilient systems that power modern applications.

My expertise spans from designing event-driven architectures to implementing high-performance APIs that handle thousands of requests per second. I'm particularly passionate about Go, Python and Java ecosystems, distributed consensus algorithms, and building systems that gracefully handle failure.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me making music, playing guitar and piano, or singing. I've found that music and technology both require creativity, precision, and a deep understanding of complex systems.
